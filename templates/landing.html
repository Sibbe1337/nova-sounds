Yes, you own 100% of the content you create with our platform. You're free to use the videos on YouTube or any other platform without attribution to us.

Can I upload my own music?

Absolutely! While we provide a library of trending, royalty-free tracks, you can upload your own music in MP3, WAV, or FLAC format. Just make sure you have the rights to use the music you upload.

What's the video quality like?

All videos are generated in full HD (1080x1920) resolution optimized for vertical viewing on mobile devices. Our Creator and Agency plans also offer 4K resolution options.
